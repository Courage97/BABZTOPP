ENGR. OJEDIYA JOSHUA BABARINDE

CEO

Engr. Ojediya Joshua Babarinde is the visionary CEO of Babztopp Homes and Properties. Born in Alokunsoro, Ogbomoso, Oyo State, Joshua's educational journey began at Aunty Dupe Nursery and Primary School in Ile Oluji, Ondo State. He continued to excel at Homaj International Secondary School in Ondo Town, where he obtained his School Leaving Certificate. Joshua then pursued higher education at The Polytechnic, Ibadan, earning a Higher National Diploma in Insurance. With a strong foundation in risk management and financial planning, Joshua embarked on his entrepreneurial journey by investing his time, energy, and resources into a mini transportation business. This venture honed his business skills and laid the groundwork for his future endeavors. In 2019, Joshua shifted his focus to real estate, founding Babztopp Homes and Properties. His innovative approach and commitment to excellence quickly established the company as a reputable name in the industry. Known for their client-centric services and high-quality projects, Babztopp Homes and Properties has flourished under Joshua's leadership. Engr. Joshua's dedication, integrity, and strategic vision continue to drive the company's success, making him a respected figure in the real estate sector. His story is a testament to the power of hard work, perseverance, and visionary leadership.
